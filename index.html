My Work

Quick Aid

Quick Aid is a responsive emergency contact web platform designed to store critical personal and vehicle information securely.

Users can register, manage multiple emergency profiles, and generate unique Aztec codes linked to their data.

When scanned,these codes display essential details to bystanders, enabling timely help in emergencies.

The system enforces strict data validation to prevent unauthorized use of vehicle numbers and emergency contacts.

Built with Flask, PostgreSQL, HTML/CSS/JavaScript, and hosted on Render, it ensures accessibility across all devices.
